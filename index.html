TRAVELING YEAR

Between July 2019 to November 2019, I've been on a plane 15 times.

An alley in Shanghai, China where residents hang their sheets to dry.

Here's some places I've been to:

  • July 2019: I left to go work in Seattle for Kode With Klossy
  • August 2019: I went to Hawaii to film a wedding
  • August 2019: I traveled to Los Angeles for my first time in California
  • September 2019: I moved to Shanghai, China for the semester to attend Fudan University
  • October 2019: For Chinese National Week, I traveled to Korea with my friends
  • October 2019: I attended Devcon V as a scholar in Japan
  • October 2019: I went back to America due to visa complications
  • October 2019: I arrived back in Shanghai
  • November 2019: I flew to Zhuhai, Guangdong to attend EDC China 2019
